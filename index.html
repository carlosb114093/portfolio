Projects

Kynophobia

Carlos is a boy with a severe phobia of dogs (kynophobia). At night, his fear haunts him in his dreams where he must escape a relentless dog on a suspended zigzag path.

Genre: Endless Runner

Engine: Unity

Platform: PC

Implemented Features:

  • Automatic forward scrolling.
  • Press space bar to turn 90° left or right.
  • Escape from a nightmare (a German Shepherd!) and avoid falling.

Parasite Invasion

You find yourself inside the human body, piloting an elite ship designed to eradicate viruses and parasites. Waves of hostile microorganisms fire infectious projectiles, draining life from the body.

Genre: Shoot 'em up

Engine: Unity

Platform: PC

Implemented Features:

  • Microscopic battle setting inside the human body.
  • Immersive visuals inspired by the immune system.
  • Challenging gameplay that requires strategy and reflexes.
